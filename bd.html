My Love

And when the sun shines dear,
I open my eyes for another day.
I take a breath,
And another one.
With a sigh I begin my day,
And for me there is no other way.

Eat my breakfast, drink the tea,
Running fast to catch the bus.
The morning sun, still unforgiving,
Blazing down its sharp sun rays.

In a moment, I am myself,
Broken from this collar of serfdom.
Suddenly thoughts rush to my mind,
And all of them filled with your image.

Your smile, so bright and warm,
Your eyes tender and forgiving,
Your words beautiful and calming,
Your lips flowing with passion.

Your hands, soft and precious,
Your legs, divine in their grace,
Your hair flowing with strength,
Your body the image of beauty

Oh, your words, and your laughter so dear,
The stories that I yearn to hear.
The songs that breath life unto me,
Your dreams that gave reason to me.

All the days and nights with you,
All the moment yearning for you.
Every day I pray for you dear,
Every moment I miss you dear.

And the days so numbered,
Nights even less.
All the things that keep us tied,
I so dearly wish break loose,
And such we can spend some time.

My love, my muse, my dear, darling,
Lady so beautiful,
Words fail their duty.
What can I ever have done to have you,
You my dear, my breath my heart,
Darling I'm blessed for you are here.

How much I thank god,
It is never enough.
Every day I say my prayers,
For I thank god for you.

My darling my love,
I love you dear.
And I am forever thankful,
That you are here.

A wondrous new year to you,
Happy birthday,
For a new chapter,
New story.
